About EventMITILENCE, is a prestigious annual event hosted by our Manakula Vinayagar Institute of Technology. Its primary objective is to ignite students' talents and offer them opportunities to explore the latest technological advancements. MITILENCE provides a platform for students to exhibit their expertise in coding, design, innovation, and problem-solving while also participating in engaging and creative activities. The event showcases a vibrant blend of technical and non-technical competitions, fostering learning, teamwork, and enthusiasm among all participants.
